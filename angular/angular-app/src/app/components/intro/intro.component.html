E91 Protocol Introduction

In cryptography, Ciphers are algorithms which describe steps for encrypting and decrypting information. Modern ciphers include the secure transmission of a key which is sent with the plaintext and the encryption algorithm. The main problem of modern cryptographic systems is the distribution among the participants of the communication session of a secret key, possession of which should not be available to third parties. In principle transmitting messages using classical channels allows an eavesdropper to gain information about the key without being detected. However, constructing transmission techniques based on quantum mechanics allows parties to transmit messages and detect the presence of an eavesdropper every time. ​
Proposed in 1991 by Artur Ekert, E91 protocol is an entanglement based protocol which uses Bell states shared between Alice and Bob for secure distribution of secret key .

​
  • The sender (Alice) and receiver (Bob) share |ψ-⟩ bell state. Alice makes a measurement with a direction randomly chosen Horizontal (X observable),Diagonal (W observable),Vertical (Z observable). Whereas Bob makes a measurement with a direction randomly chosen between Diagonal (W observable),Vertical (Z observable), Anti Diagonal (V observable). Alice and Bob record the measurement results and broadcast the measurement basis which they used, through the classical channel .If Alice and Bob have measured the spin projections of the entangled pair of qubits onto the same direction (i.e. W--W or Z--Z for Alice's and Bob's qubit respectively), then they are sure that they obtained opposite results. Thus, if Alice got the result ±1, then Bob definitely will get the result ∓1, i.e. the results will be perfectly anticorrelated. Both Alice and Bob agree on that the measurement +1 represents the classical bit 1, while the measurement –1 represents the classical bit 0, and thus get their key string.​
  • Suppose some third party wants to interfere in the communication session of Alice and Bob and obtain a secret key. The eavesdropper can use the intercept-resend attacks: Eve intercepts one or both of the entangled qubits, measures the spin projections of these qubits, prepares new ones depending on the results obtained (|01⟩ or |10⟩) and sends them to Alice and Bob.

E2E Protocol Introduction


E2E (End to End entanglement) application is related to generate n-entangled pair of a target fidelity between two nodes, the source and the destination, those can be present anywhere in a given topology.



The steps of the application is following-​

  1. Topology of the quantum Network will be prepared 
  2. Request of n- entangled pairs of target fidelity F, between source (S) and Destination (D) raised by user.
  3. A routing algorithm will choose best path according the resource availability of the intermediate node between source (S) and Destination (D).
  4. Required resources will be reserved on the selected path.
  5. Entanglement generation between the adjacent nodes.
  6. Entanglement Purification for the increasing fidelity of the generated (will be repeated after Swapping as well)​
  7. Entanglement Swapping at the intermediate nodes
  8. End to end entanglement will be completed

Teleportation Protocol Introduction



The sender (Alice) wants to send quantum information (qubit state |ψ⟩=α|0⟩+β|1⟩) to receiver (Bob). Due to No-cloning theorem Alice cannot simply make an exact copy of an unknown quantum state and give the copy to Bob. We can only copy classical states (not superpositions).To transfer a quantum bit, Alice and Bob must share an entangled qubit pair. Alice then performs a Hadamard operation followed by a CNOT gate between a qubit it wants to send/teleport and the entangled qubit at Alice’s end . And then she measures the qubits out, which is called Bell-state measurement and sends the measurement results to Bob over a classical communication channel, After Bob receives the message and he applies a corrective unitary operations (based on the results data that he gets from Alice ) on his entangled part of the qubit. The updated qubit that bob has now is the qubit that was at Alice’s end initially .We call this teleportation because, at the end , Bob will have |ψ⟩ and Alice won't anymore.
